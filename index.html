Studio Details

When should I book my session?
Maternity is best at 28-34 weeks. For Newborns, we recommend booking during your second trimester to ensure availability.
Do you provide all outfits and props?
Yes, we have a curated luxury collection of maternity gowns, baby wraps, outfits, and props. You just need to arrive and relax.
Is your studio safe for newborns?
Absolutely. We maintain a medical-grade hygienic, temperature-controlled environment and Deepti is a certified safe-posing specialist.
Do you cover events outside Hyderabad?
Yes, we travel for premium event bookings and home sessions across Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and Karnataka.
